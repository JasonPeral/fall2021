Crypto Currency Mining

What is crypto mining?

The term crypto mining means gaining cryptocurrencies by solving cryptographic equations through the use of computers.
This process involves validating data blocks and adding transaction records to a public record (ledger) known as a blockchain.
In a more technical sense, cryptocurrency mining is a transactional process that involves the use of computers and cryptographic processes to solve complex
functions and record data to a blockchain. In fact, there are entire networks of devices that are involved in cryptomining and that keep shared records via those blockchains.

Is crypto mining legal?

Legality depends on 2 factors but for the most part crypto mining is legal.

  1. Geographical location
  2. If you mine through illegal means
Illegal means of crypto mining can be cyber hacking using programming or installing malware on unsuspecting users and jacking processing power to acquire
quicker mining speeds.

What you need to get started mining(basics)

Buy the right computer hardware

To start mining you need the right hardware. One of the most important part of a mining rig is the graphics card which is where the most of the processing power will come from.
The more processing power you have the more crypto you can get rewarded with.

  • Motherboard
  • Cpu
  • Power supply
  • RAM
  • Most importantly: Graphics Card

Setup an adequate cooling system

Since mining hardware releases a lot of energy in the form of heat, it is important to adopt ways to cool the system down.
Electric cooling fans and air fans placed next to the hardware can help you solve this problem.

Setup a crypto wallet

Cryptocurrency exchanges such as Coinbase can be used to open an account where you will receive the rewards you earn from mining.

Download a mining software and join a mining pool

There are different mining softwares available for mining different cryptocurrencies. Once you find a legitimate one, connect it to your hardware.
